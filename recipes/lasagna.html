Lasagna

A hearty and flavorful Italian classic, this lasagna layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets, topped with melted cheese. Perfectly baked to golden perfection, it’s a comforting dish ideal for family dinners or special gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or fresh)
  • 500g ground beef or pork (or a mix)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add ground meat, cook until browned. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare béchamel sauce by melting butter, whisking in flour for 2 minutes, then gradually adding milk. Cook until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  4. Layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese in a baking dish. Repeat until ingredients are used, ending with a cheese layer.
  5. Bake for 30–40 minutes, until golden and bubbling.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
